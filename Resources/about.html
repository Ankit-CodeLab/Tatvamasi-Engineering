About Us

Tatvamasi Engineering was founded in 2018 by Mr.Parag Babariya and Mr.Amit Paghdar. Company is working in precise machining sector since 2005 as Phoenix Machining Works. From the beginning, we have been committed to providing our customers with quality products, competitive pricing and excellent service, is backed with an experience of 15 years in the field of Oil Engine Parts, Automobile Parts, Textile Parts, Tractor Parts, Marin Parts - have grown today to a company of high repute for manufacturing, developing and suppling of precision products. Growing from strength to strength our image has reached the zenith of stature backed by a great team of Workers, Engineers, Inspectors and Management Staff. Tatvamasi Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is following consistently high standards of quality have resulted in acceptance of product with wide range of customers both in Indian as well as in overseas markets. With our stress on R&D and continuous refinement of production methods Tatvamasi Engineering Pvt. Ltd. is led by highly qualified technocrats with wide experience in their areas of operation, thus providing an ideal combination of man and machine.
